Julia Kerschbaum is an art director and graphic designer with a focus on editorial design and corporate identities. Always having Austria and the mountains in her heart, she’s now based in Hamburg. Since 2013 Juls is a loving member of the loved family, working for clients like Audi, Clubstiftung, Quantum and Interpack.

She’s a car enthusiast, is crazy for handlettering and loves infographics.
